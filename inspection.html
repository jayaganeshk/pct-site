Inspection Services

PCT Group Ltd provides lifting equipment recertification and test inspection to all types of industry across the Middle East. Our inspectors are certified across many disciplines with the lifting Equipment Engineers Association (LEEA) Our standards in providing this service are second to none. Our engineers and qualified mechanical engineers by trade and undergo stringent training and qualification from LEEA, who are the industry leaders. PCT Group is a full member of LEEA, along with ISO 9001:2015 certification and accreditation from ISO/IEC 17020:2012.

Available Inspection Services

Inspection for all types of Lifting Accessories such as wire rope slings, webbing slings, chain slings, shackles, eyebolts, clamps, lifting beams, hooks, link assemblies, etc.
Crane Inspection for Mobile, EOTC, Gantries, Pedestal and Crawler cranes
Manual Lifting Equipment Inspections such as Chain Blocks, Lever Hoists, Jacks, Pallet Lifters, etc.
Powered Lifting Equipment inspections such as Electric, Pneumatic and Hydraulic Winches, etc.
Industrial Fork Lift Inspection
General Pad Eye Testing Using High Capacity Hydraulic Jacks
Frames Units, Offshore Container and Storage Bin Load Testing General SWL Verification and Certification Man Riding Baskets
