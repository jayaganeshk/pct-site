Who We are

The PCT GROUP of companies have been involved in the materials handling industry since the companies inception in 1972. During this period, we acquired Coubro & Scrutton, Matterson Cranes and King Hoists who are some of the oldest and most respected manufacturers in the lifting industry. We also own Victory Inc.  based in Saint- Etienne France, where a number of standard & specialized products are manufactured.

The Group has offices and factories in Glasgow (head office), Aberdeen & Rochdale, we also have manufacturing operations in Saint-Etienne France, Dubai UAE, & offices in Baku & Saudi Arabia, from where we supply our  worldwide client base.

Our own in-house cad design department, comprehensive machine shop and fabrication facilities, affords us full control of all aspects of build quality & production scheduling. The Group has full ISO 9001: 2015 Accreditation , coupled with our quality control /inspection department, ensures that we have full compliance with International Standards.

Manufactured Products

We have been recognised as specialists in designing products specifically to match the ever increasing demands of the energy and construction sectors.

We manufacture bespoke lifting solutions, Ex compliant Hoists, Overhead Cranes, Powered and Manual Chain Blocks with excellent headroom capabilities & close radius performance, up to a capacity of 80 metric tones. In Ex configuration we also have a Unique Hoisting System for use in LNG pump handling.

Together with our extensive range of standard lifting equipment, i.e. Manual & Powered Hoists, Winches, Cranes & Lifting accessories, we can provide the most comprehensive package possible to meet your Material Handling requirements.
