PROLIFT AIR HOISTS “PA”

The unique “PROLIFT” range of vane-motor air chain hoists have a 100% duty cycle, anti-friction bearings and are remarkably compact. The speed of operation is infinitely variable, from precise inching to high-speed hoisting. The motor is suitable for environments requiring spark-resistant qualities. The chain guide gives maximum support with minimum chain wear and each hoist is fitted with an air-actuated, brake for long life. With unitized construction and a 5: 1 factor of safety, the “PROLIFT” vane-motor air hoists represents the optimum in efficiency, safety and value. All hoists are fitted with Grade 80 heat-treated and case-hardened load chain for long life, together with fully swiveling forged steel hooks.

PROLIFT AIR HOISTS “MAMMOTH”

“PROLIFT” MAMMOTH air chain hoists with piston or gear motors have many features in their compact design which give ultimate operating efficiency and safety. These include fall-safe brakes, positive valve control with emergency shut-off, alloy steel hooks with safety catches and roller swivel bearings. Limit over-travel top and bottom is prevented by limit switches and each block is fitted with lifting lugs to facilitate installation. All “PROLIFT” MAMMOTH air chain hoists are fitted with efficient exhaust silencers and the trollies can be fitted with motor drive. Optional extras include special suspension arrangements. Chain buckets and air supply festoons. Spark and corrosion resistant models are available for use in hazardous environments. Lifting speeds which vary form 0.6 metres per minute to 3 metres per minute according to hoist capacity, coupled with the standard (1.5m) pendant control allow for load location with the greatest precision, one of the many features which has been made the “PROLIFT” MAMMOTH air hoist increasingly popular with all concerned with the high demands of industry world-wide.

WORMGEAR WINCHES “PW”

 The design of “PROLIFT” wormgear winches is based on two important factors: sturdiness and economy. The use of high quality components and robust construction ensures a long life. Wormgear winches are best suited for intermittent use. Where high duty factors are encountered, planetary winches are preferable. Standard wormgear winches are selfbraking, for general hoisting purposes however a brake motor is recommended.
It is useful to consider a running in period of approx 24hrs by limiting the load to 70% of the maximum line-pull mentioned in this catalogue.
