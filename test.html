LOADWISE MODEL 800

 

 

 

 

LOADWISE MODEL 800 RATED CAPACITY & LOAD MOMENT INDICATOR

 

The 800 series is a fully automatic microprocessor controlled system and operational aid that clearly displays the information and warnings needed for the driver to operate the crane within the parameters of the duty chart.   Designed to meet the requirements of smaller cranes and those with typically up to twenty load charts the Loadwise Model 800 provides a basic system at an economic price. All the units electronics and external connections are housed in the display enclosure.  

Key features of the 800 series include: -  

 

*

 Digital LCD display (25mm x 76mm), vertical format - all essential information continuously displayed includes: - 

*

 Duty configuration selected

*

 Hoist drum (winch) selected and the number of lines (falls) reeved on the hook block 

*

 Rated Capacity or maximum permitted load

*

 Actual load lifted

*

 Bar graph indicating the actual load lifted as a percentage of the rated capacity

*

 Radius of the load 

*

 Angle of the boom

 

*

 Visual and audible warnings of approach to and overload conditions

 

*

 Warning messages e.g. Out of radius, Cut out, High angle, Out of level, Overhoist, Last winding (Rope on Winch/drum)

*

 Length of the boom

*

 Height of the boom head

*

 Operator adjustable limits with audible warnings 

*

 Displays information & warning messages e.g. "OVERHOIST" 

*

 Rated Capacity Limiter to existing crane motion controls

Options: -   

 

*

 Anti 2 block (A2B) overhoist limit facility when the optional limit switch assembly is fitted - Loadwise Model 330 

*

 Anemometer (wind speed) information can be displayed and limit set
